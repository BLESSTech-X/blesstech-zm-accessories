Our Journey

From a Single WhatsApp Chat to Hundreds of Happy Customers

It started with a single WhatsApp conversation. A friend needed a phone case and couldn't find a good one at a fair price in Lusaka. We sourced one, delivered it the same day, and that was the beginning of BLESSTech-ZM.

Today, we carry over 50 products — from premium cases and fast chargers to wireless earbuds and power banks. Every product is hand-selected for quality, every price is set fairly, and every order is handled personally.

We believe Zambians deserve the same quality phone accessories available to anyone in the world — without overpaying or waiting weeks. That belief drives everything we do.
