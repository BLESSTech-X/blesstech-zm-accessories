Who We Are

From Lusaka, for Lusaka

BLESSTech-ZM Accessories was born out of frustration — the frustration of walking into a store and being sold fake chargers, cheap cases that crack in a week, and cables that stop working after a month. Barack and Sylvester knew there had to be a better way.

We built BLESSTech-ZM on a simple promise: every product we sell is genuine, every price is fair, and every customer gets the kind of personal attention you'd get from a trusted friend who happens to know everything about phone accessories.

Today we serve hundreds of customers across Lusaka through WhatsApp, delivering the same day and standing behind every product we sell. We're not just a shop — we're your go-to tech friends in Lusaka.
